To Buy or Not To Buy

You're randomly chosen to be either the Angel or Devil on my shoulders. Click the button to think of arguments against your opponent. Whoever has the most eureka (lightbulbs) convinces me for that round. Whoever wins the most arguments gets to decide if I should buy or not to buy.

If one question mark "?" gets rolled, you got lost in the sauce a bit in your argument and your opponent takes over.

If two question marks "?" gets rolled, I lost my train of thought and your current score gets zeroed out.
